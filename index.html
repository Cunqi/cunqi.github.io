Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 22, 2025
App: GalleryTok

1. Introduction

GalleryTok (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (“App”). By using GalleryTok, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

  • Photos and Videos: With your permission, GalleryTok accesses your device’s photo library to allow browsing, organizing, and detecting duplicate photos and videos.
  • App Usage Data: We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics (e.g., which features are used most often) to help us improve the App.
  • No Personal Account Required: GalleryTok does not require you to create an account or provide personally identifiable information (such as name, email, phone number).

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide core functionality, such as browsing, organizing, and deleting photos/videos.
  • To improve the performance and user experience of the App.
  • To support customer inquiries and technical assistance.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All photo and video analysis is performed locally on your device. Your media is never uploaded to our servers.
  • If the App uses iCloud (optional), your data is stored securely in Apple’s cloud infrastructure.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may use Apple services such as:

  • StoreKit for in-app purchases.
  • iCloud (if enabled) for syncing across devices.

These services are subject to Apple’s privacy policies.

6. Your Choices

  • You can revoke the App’s access to your photo library at any time in your device settings.
  • You can delete the App to remove all locally stored data.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected with a new “Effective Date.”
